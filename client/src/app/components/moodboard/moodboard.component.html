Mood boards play a crucial role in your home renovation process. They serve as a creative platform to gather and organize inspiration from various sources such as show units, open source images, furniture catalogues, paint and fabric swatches, and allow you to visually explore and experiment with different design concepts, styles, color schemes, and materials. Mood boards provide designers a reference for creating cohesive and visually appealing interior designs, and aid in communicating your design ideas with collaborators.

Pinterest
Find inspiration, create mood boards effortlessly on Pinterest and organize your projects into multiple boards
Evernote
Create separate notebooks for different projects and add images to the notebook to make your own mood board
Canva
Search images within Canva and add them to your collage mood boards using the app's templates and stickers
Adobe Illustrator
Exercise extensive design flexibility in creating your mood boards if you have access to the Adobe Creative Cloud
Moodboard
A beginner-friendly, straightforward interface that offers you templates or starter mood boards to choose from
Foyr Neo
Create mood boards with AI-assisted 3D rendering, specifically built for the needs of interior designers
